# NEMOIS: Square World, Fluid Experiences

Hi, we are Team NEMOIS.

> "The world is square, but the experiences within it should flow smoothly."

We live in a world of sharp angles—rectangular buildings, square screens, and rigid structures. Yet, human nature naturally gravitates toward soft, organic curves. 

NEMOIS bridges these two worlds. We believe that even within the rigid frames of technology, human-centered experiences can flow naturally and seamlessly. This belief is the foundation of everything we do.

---

## Our Philosophy
Grounded in minimalism, we focus on three core human-centered values:

### 1. The Power of Space
Space isn’t just empty room; it is where the mind finds rest. By stripping away the unnecessary, we create meaningful space for users to think, breathe, and fill with their own thoughts.

### 2. Restrained Expression
We choose clarity over clutter. By distilling complex information into its simplest form, we reveal the core message. When design is intuitive and effortless, our message resonates deepest.

### 3. Refined Experience
True simplicity means hiding complexity behind the curtain, leaving only absolute comfort for the user. We design seamless flows so users can reach their goals without friction. Effortless journeys build lasting trust.

---
NEMOIS infuses rigid structures with fluid, human values.
